About

This is the blog of Stephen Haberman.

After years of thinking I did not have much to contribute, my ego has finally convinced me otherwise. Whether or not this is true remains to be seen.

I was a co-founder of Exigence, though lately I've been enjoying working for Bizo.

I enjoy software development a little too much. It makes me good at what I do, but it also brings out some of my draconian tendencies.

I'm on github and LinkedIn. I have a handful of open source projects, but Joist and Tessell are the primary ones.
