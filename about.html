About

This is the blog of Stephen Haberman.

After years of not having much to contribute, my ego has finally convinced me otherwise. Whether or not this is true remains to be seen.

I'm a co-founder of Exigence, though lately I've been enjoying working for Bizo.

I enjoy software development a little too much. It makes me good at what I do, but it also brings out some of my draconian overlord tendencies.

I'm on github and LinkedIn. I also started the Joist open source project, but it's waiting for some real-world projects to drive its development.
